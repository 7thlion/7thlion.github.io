How We Work

Gxpress Solutions (India) Pvt. Ltd. offers a complete solution for International shipping and logistics services for B2B, B2C, & C2C shippers. Our products range from International Door to door solutions (DDS), full container load (FCL), less than container load (LCL), ocean freight services, air freight services, international e-commerce product distribution solutions, Fulfillment Solutions, warehousing, small parcels-Docs & Non docs. We customize our service levels based on your business needs.

Pickup

We do not hold and consolidate shipment material. We coordinate the shipment pickup from your facility.

Custom Clearance

We transport your material to one of the four U.S. TSA Certified processing facilities

Freight

Once your material arrives at our facility, we process same-day, for next day departure.

Delivery

We have developed Work-Share partnerships with U.S. and International Postal Authorities and Agents, to assist with last mile delivery.
